## 1. Purpose & Philosophy

### 1.1 Why Metrics Matter

For JoyMatcher, success is not measured by time-on-app or swipes-per-day. Our North Star is **marriages and long-term committed relationships**.

This document defines how we:
1. **Track user success** (engagements, marriages, relationship quality)
2. **Measure marketing effectiveness** (content, social, paid campaigns)
3. **Monitor business health** (acquisition, retention, revenue)
4. **Present data** to stakeholders (dashboards, reports, presentations)

### 1.2 Measurement Philosophy

**Core Principles:**

1. **Outcomes over Vanity Metrics**
   - ❌ Total users, page views, social media followers (alone)
   - ✅ Engagements, marriages, Premium conversions, user satisfaction

2. **Quality over Quantity**
   - ❌ Maximizing sign-ups at any cost
   - ✅ Attracting serious, marriage-minded professionals

3. **Long-Term over Short-Term**
   - ❌ Monthly active users (platform optimizes for leaving when successful)
   - ✅ Success rate, time-to-engagement, relationship longevity

4. **Transparency over Spin**
   - ❌ Cherry-picking data to look good
   - ✅ Honest reporting of wins and challenges

5. **Action-Oriented over Passive Reporting**
   - ❌ Dashboards no one reviews
   - ✅ Insights that drive decisions

## 2. Success Story Metrics

### 2.1 Collection & Tracking

**Definition of Success:**
- **Engagement**: User reports engagement (via profile update, deactivation survey, or testimonial submission)
- **Marriage**: User reports marriage
- **Long-Term Relationship**: User reports committed, marriage-oriented relationship of 6+ months

**Collection Methods:**

**1. Automated Triggers**
- **Account deactivation survey**: "Why are you leaving?" → Option: "I found my person"
- **Relationship status update**: Profile field change to "Engaged" or "Married"
- **Automated follow-up email**: 6 months after matching with someone (if still active together)

**2. Manual Reporting**
- **Success story submission form**: On website, in-app
- **User emails**: Direct messages to team
- **Social media tags**: Users tagging @joymatcher in engagement/wedding posts

**3. VIP Coordinator Reports**
- VIP Concierge tracks outcomes of curated introductions
- Manual entry into CRM/database

**Tracking Database:**
- **CRM or Airtable**: Central database for all success stories
- **Fields**:
  - User 1 ID and Name
  - User 2 ID and Name
  - Match Date
  - First Meeting Date
  - Relationship Status (Dating, Engaged, Married)
  - Engagement/Marriage Date
  - Story Consent Status (Pending, Approved, Declined)
  - Publication Status (Unpublished, Published)
  - Source (Survey, VIP Report, User Submission)
  - Subscription Tier (Free, Premium, VIP)
  - Location(s)
  - Tags (Diaspora, Cross-Border, Same-City, etc.)

### 2.2 Key Success Metrics

**Primary KPIs:**

| Metric | Definition | Target | Tracking Frequency |
|--------|------------|--------|-------------------|
| **Engagement Rate** | % of users who get engaged within 24 months of joining | 20%+ | Quarterly |
| **Marriage Rate** | % of users who get married within 36 months of joining | 15%+ | Quarterly |
| **Success Rate (Overall)** | % of users who report finding long-term committed partner | 40%+ | Quarterly |
| **Time to Engagement** | Median months from match to engagement | <12 months | Quarterly |
| **Success Stories Collected** | # of success stories collected per quarter | 30-40 | Monthly |
| **Success Story Consent Rate** | % of successful users who consent to story publication | 60%+ | Quarterly |

**Secondary Metrics:**

| Metric | Definition | Target | Tracking Frequency |
|--------|------------|--------|-------------------|
| **VIP Success Rate** | % of VIP users who find partner within 12 months | 85%+ | Monthly |
| **Premium Success Rate** | % of Premium users who find partner within 12 months | 40%+ | Monthly |
| **Free User Success Rate** | % of Free users who find partner within 12 months | 10-15% | Monthly |
| **Cross-Border Success Stories** | # of success stories involving different countries | Track trend | Quarterly |
| **Average Relationship Length (Before Marriage)** | Median months from first date to marriage | 12-18 months | Annually |

**Data Sources:**
- User surveys (deactivation, post-engagement, post-marriage)
- Relationship status updates (profile)
- Success story submissions
- VIP Coordinator reports
- Email responses

### 2.3 Cohort Analysis

**Cohort Definition:** Users who joined in a specific month/quarter

**Track by Cohort:**
- **Month 3**: % who completed Tier 2+
- **Month 6**: % who had first meaningful match
- **Month 12**: % who found long-term partner
- **Month 24**: % who got engaged
- **Month 36**: % who got married

**Example:**
```
Q1 2025 Cohort (500 users):
- Month 3: 60% completed Tier 2
- Month 6: 40% had meaningful match
- Month 12: 35% found long-term partner
- Month 24: 22% engaged
- Month 36: 18% married
```

**Analysis:**
- Compare cohorts over time (is success rate improving?)
- Identify patterns (which user segments succeed fastest?)
- Adjust strategy (what predicts success?)

## 5. User Satisfaction & Qualitative Metrics

### 5.1 Net Promoter Score (NPS)

**Definition:** "On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend JoyMatcher to a friend?"

**Segments:**
- **Promoters (9-10)**: Loyal, enthusiastic users
- **Passives (7-8)**: Satisfied but unenthusiastic
- **Detractors (0-6)**: Unhappy users

**NPS Score:** % Promoters - % Detractors

**Target:** 50+ (Excellent)

**Survey Frequency:**
- **All users**: Quarterly in-app survey
- **Premium users**: After 3 months of subscription
- **Post-success users**: After reporting engagement/marriage

**Follow-Up:**
- Ask "Why?" to understand score
- Thank Promoters, ask for testimonial or referral
- Address Detractors' concerns, offer support

### 5.2 User Satisfaction Surveys

**Survey Types:**

**1. Post-Sign-Up Survey (Day 7)**
- "How easy was it to create your profile?" (1-5 scale)
- "How satisfied are you with the initial matches?" (1-5 scale)
- "What would make JoyMatcher better for you?" (Open-ended)

**Target:** 4+ avg. rating

**2. Post-Upgrade Survey (Premium, Day 30)**
- "Has Premium been worth the investment?" (Yes/No/Too early to tell)
- "What Premium feature do you use most?" (Multiple choice)
- "What would you improve?" (Open-ended)

**Target:** 80%+ say "Yes" or "Too early"

**3. Deactivation Survey (All Users)**
- "Why are you leaving?" (Multiple choice + open-ended)
  - Options: Found my person, Not enough matches, Too expensive, Taking a break, Other
- If "Found my person": Trigger success story workflow

**Target:** 40%+ leaving because they found partner

**4. Post-Success Survey (Engaged/Married Users)**
- "How satisfied are you with your JoyMatcher experience?" (1-5 scale)
- "How well did JoyMatcher prepare you for a serious relationship?" (1-5 scale)
- "Would you recommend JoyMatcher to others?" (Yes/No)
- "Would you be willing to share your story?" (Yes/No)

**Target:** 4.5+ avg. rating, 90%+ would recommend

### 5.3 Qualitative Feedback

**Sources:**
- Open-ended survey responses
- Customer support tickets
- Social media comments and DMs
- User interviews (quarterly, 10-15 users)
- VIP Coordinator feedback (from client conversations)

**Analysis:**
- Thematic coding (identify recurring themes)
- Sentiment analysis (positive, neutral, negative)
- Feature requests (prioritize roadmap)
- Pain points (address in product or content)

**Quarterly Review:**
- Summarize top 5 themes (positive and negative)
- Present to product and marketing teams
- Create action plan

## 9. Data Privacy & Ethics

### 9.1 User Consent for Data Use

**Success Story Data:**
- Always obtain explicit consent before publishing (see Testimonials System doc)
- Users control what's shared (names, photos, location detail)
- Users can revoke consent anytime

**Aggregate Data:**
- General statistics (e.g., "40% of Premium users find partners") do NOT require individual consent
- Anonymized and aggregated only

**Analytics Data:**
- Use for internal decision-making and reporting
- Do NOT sell or share with third parties
- GDPR/NDPR compliant

### 9.2 Ethical Reporting

**Do NOT:**
- ❌ Cherry-pick data to misrepresent success (e.g., only show VIP success rate)
- ❌ Manipulate definitions to inflate metrics (e.g., "success" = first date)
- ❌ Publish success stories without consent
- ❌ Use user data for purposes users didn't consent to

**DO:**
- ✅ Report honestly (wins and challenges)
- ✅ Provide context for metrics (e.g., "VIP success rate is higher because of concierge service")
- ✅ Respect user privacy at all times
- ✅ Be transparent about how data is collected and used
