## 4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR/NDPR)

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

### 4.1 Contractual Necessity
Processing is necessary to provide the matchmaking service you signed up for.
- **Examples:** Account creation, profile display, tier calculation, messaging

### 4.2 Consent
You explicitly consented to processing for specific purposes.
- **Examples:** Marketing emails, Tier 5 identity verification, testimonials, data sharing with VIP experts

**You may withdraw consent at any time** without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

### 4.3 Legitimate Interests
Processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests, balanced against your privacy rights.
- **Examples:** Analytics, fraud detection, platform security, improving matchmaking algorithms

### 4.4 Legal Obligation
Processing is required to comply with laws and regulations.
- **Examples:** Responding to court orders, data breach notifications, tax compliance

## 6. Data Storage & Security

### 6.1 Where We Store Your Data

#### Primary Data Centers
- **Nigerian users:** Data stored in Nigeria
- **International users:** Data stored in [AWS region, e.g., Ireland for EU users]

#### Backup Locations
- Encrypted backups in [backup region]
- Retained for disaster recovery only

### 6.2 How Long We Store Your Data

#### Active Accounts
- Indefinitely while your account is active
- Regular backups retained for 90 days

#### Deleted Accounts
- **Grace period:** 30 days (allows reactivation)
- **After 30 days:** Personal data deleted, remaining data anonymized

#### Anonymized Data
- Retained for 7 years (legal compliance, statistical analysis)
- Cannot be traced back to you

#### Specific Data Retention
| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Profile data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Messages | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Payment records | 7 years (tax compliance) |
| Audit logs (admin actions) | 7 years (legal compliance) |
| Support tickets | 3 years |
| Marketing email consent | Until withdrawn + 30 days |
| VIP expert notes | Until VIP membership ends + 1 year |
| Tier 5 KYC documents | Deleted 90 days after VIP membership ends |

### 6.3 Security Measures

#### Encryption
- **Data in transit:** TLS 1.3 encryption (HTTPS)
- **Data at rest:** AES-256 encryption for sensitive fields (passwords, Tier 5 documents)
- **Passwords:** Bcrypt hashing (cost factor: 12)

#### Access Controls
- Role-based access control (RBAC) for admins
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) for sensitive admin roles
- Principle of least privilege (minimum permissions needed)

#### Monitoring & Auditing
- Real-time intrusion detection
- Regular vulnerability scans
- Penetration testing (annually)
- Audit logs of all admin actions

#### Infrastructure Security
- Firewalls and DDoS protection
- Regular security patches
- Secure API endpoints
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse

#### VIP Expert Isolation
- VIP experts can ONLY query assigned clients (database-level enforcement)
- No platform-wide user browsing
- All actions audited

### 6.4 Security Limitations

**No system is 100% secure.** You acknowledge:
- Data transmission over the internet carries inherent risks
- You are responsible for safeguarding your password
- We are not liable for unauthorized access due to your negligence

## 7. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR/NDPR/CCPA)

### 7.1 Right to Access

**You have the right to request a copy of your personal data.**

**How to request:**
- Navigate to [Account Settings > Data Export](app/account/data-export)
- Or email: dpo@joymatcher.com

**We provide:**
- All personal data we hold about you
- Categories of data collected
- Purposes of processing
- Third parties who received your data

**Format:** JSON or PDF
**Delivery time:** Within 30 days (GDPR/NDPR), within 45 days (CCPA)

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Correction)

**You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.**

**How to correct:**
- Update your profile directly from [Edit Profile](app/profile/me/edit)
- For data you cannot edit (email, account creation date), email: dpo@joymatcher.com

### 7.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten")

**You have the right to request deletion of your personal data.**

**How to request:**
- Navigate to [Account Settings > Delete Account](app/account/delete-account)
- Or email: dpo@joymatcher.com

**What gets deleted:**
- Your name, email, phone number
- Profile photos and content
- Messages and conversation history
- Show Interest history
- Tier data
- VIP intake responses

**What gets anonymized (not deleted):**
- Statistical data (e.g., "User X completed Tier 3 on Date Y" becomes "Anonymous user completed Tier 3 on Date Y")
- Transaction records (required for tax compliance, retained for 7 years)
- Audit logs (admin actions, retained for 7 years)

**Exceptions (we may refuse deletion if):**
- Required to comply with legal obligations
- Needed to defend legal claims
- Necessary for public interest (e.g., ongoing fraud investigation)

**Timeline:** Deletion within 30 days; anonymization within 90 days

See [Right to Deletion](right_to_deletion.md) for full technical procedures.

### 7.4 Right to Data Portability

**You have the right to receive your data in a machine-readable format.**

**How to request:**
- Navigate to [Account Settings > Data Export](app/account/data-export)
- Choose format: JSON, CSV, or PDF

**What you receive:**
- Profile data (all tiers)
- Messages (text only)
- Interaction history (interests sent/received, tier access granted)
- Subscription history

**We do NOT provide:**
- Data of other users (e.g., profiles you viewed)
- Internal analytics or scoring
- Admin notes or moderation logs

### 7.5 Right to Object to Processing

**You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.**

**Examples:**
- Object to marketing emails (use unsubscribe link)
- Object to analytics tracking (disable in [Account Settings > Privacy](app/account/privacy))
- Object to data sharing with VIP experts (downgrade from VIP)

**How to object:** Email dpo@joymatcher.com

### 7.6 Right to Restrict Processing

**You have the right to restrict processing in certain situations.**

**Examples:**
- You contest the accuracy of data (we restrict processing while verifying)
- You object to deletion and request restriction instead
- You need data for legal claims

**How to request:** Email dpo@joymatcher.com

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

**You may withdraw consent at any time** for consent-based processing.

**Examples:**
- Withdraw Tier 5 KYC consent (downgrade from VIP)
- Withdraw testimonial consent (we remove your story)
- Withdraw marketing email consent (unsubscribe)

**How to withdraw:**
- Most consents can be withdrawn from [Account Settings > Privacy](app/account/privacy)
- Or email: dpo@joymatcher.com

### 7.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

**If you believe we violated your privacy rights, you may file a complaint.**

**Nigeria:** National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA)
- Website: nitda.gov.ng
- Email: info@nitda.gov.ng

**EU:** Your national Data Protection Authority (DPA)
- Find your DPA: ec.europa.eu/justice/data-protection

**California:** California Attorney General's Office
- Website: oag.ca.gov

## 9. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

### 9.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember your preferences and activity.

### 9.2 Types of Cookies We Use

#### Essential Cookies (Cannot Opt Out)
- Session cookies (keep you logged in)
- Authentication tokens
- Security cookies (CSRF protection)

#### Functional Cookies (Can Opt Out)
- Remember your language preference
- Remember your search filters
- Remember your privacy settings

#### Analytics Cookies (Can Opt Out)
- Google Analytics (or privacy-focused alternative like Plausible)
- Measure page views, click rates, feature usage

#### Marketing Cookies (Can Opt Out)
- Track ad campaign effectiveness
- Retargeting (if you visit our website from an ad)

### 9.3 How to Manage Cookies

**Browser Settings:**
- Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
- Instructions: [Browser Help Center]

**Our Cookie Preferences:**
- Navigate to [Account Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences](app/account/privacy)
- Toggle analytics and marketing cookies on/off

### 9.4 Third-Party Cookies

We use cookies from:
- Google Analytics (analytics)
- Facebook Pixel (if running ads)
- Stripe/Paystack (payment processing)

**You can opt out of third-party tracking:**
- Google Analytics Opt-Out: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
- Facebook: facebook.com/settings?tab=ads
