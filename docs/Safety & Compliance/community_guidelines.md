**Version:** 1.0.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-02-26
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-26

## Introduction

JoyMatcher is a **trust-based matchmaking platform** designed for individuals seeking marriage or long-term committed relationships. Our community is built on mutual respect, authenticity, and shared intention.

These Community Guidelines establish behavioral expectations for all users. Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent account termination.

**Core Principle:** Treat others as you would want to be treated. Engage with honesty, respect, and empathy.

---

## 1. Our Commitment to Safety

JoyMatcher has **zero tolerance** for:
- Harassment or abuse
- Spam or scams
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Sexual exploitation
- Violence or threats
- Fraudulent behavior

**If you experience or witness violations, report immediately via:**
- In-app report button on user profiles
- [Safety Center](app/safety/report)
- Email: safety@joymatcher.com

---

## 2. Account Integrity & Authenticity

### 2.1 Be Your Authentic Self

✅ **DO:**
- Use your real name
- Provide accurate information in all tiers
- Upload recent, unedited photos of yourself
- Represent your intentions honestly

❌ **DON'T:**
- Create fake or misleading profiles
- Impersonate another person
- Use photos that aren't you
- Misrepresent your age, marital status, or intentions
- Create multiple accounts

**Why this matters:** JoyMatcher is built on trust. Authenticity is the foundation of meaningful connections.

**Consequences:** Fake profiles or misrepresentation will result in immediate account termination.

---

### 2.2 Photo Standards

✅ **Acceptable Photos:**
- Clear, recent photos (within 2 years)
- Photos showing your face clearly
- Photos of you alone (primary photo must be solo)
- Appropriate clothing (business casual or better)

❌ **Prohibited Photos:**
- Group photos as primary profile photo
- Photos with children (for privacy)
- Photos with ex-partners or romantic interests
- Heavily filtered or edited photos (minor touch-ups okay)
- Swimsuit, underwear, or revealing photos
- Photos with watermarks, logos, or promotional content
- Screenshots of other profiles
- Memes or non-photo content

**Why this matters:** Photos help users assess compatibility and build trust. Clear, honest representation is essential.

**Consequences:** Prohibited photos will be removed. Repeated violations may result in suspension.

---

### 2.3 One Account Per Person

You may only create **one account**. Multiple accounts are prohibited.

**Exceptions:** If your account was deleted or banned, you may appeal via safety@joymatcher.com. Do NOT create a new account.

**Consequences:** All duplicate accounts will be terminated.

---

## 3. Respectful Communication

### 3.1 General Communication Standards

✅ **DO:**
- Be respectful and courteous
- Communicate clearly and honestly
- Respect boundaries and consent
- Accept rejection gracefully
- Be patient with response times

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use profanity or vulgar language
- Send sexually explicit messages
- Make inappropriate comments about appearance
- Pressure others to share contact information
- Demand immediate responses

**Why this matters:** Respectful communication creates a safe, welcoming environment.

**Consequences:** Inappropriate messages may result in warnings, suspension, or termination.

---

### 3.2 Consent & Boundaries

JoyMatcher requires **explicit consent** at every stage:

**Show Interest System:**
- You **cannot** message someone unless they accept your interest
- If someone declines, respect their decision (no re-requests for 3 months)
- If someone doesn't respond, do not harass or pressure them

**Request-for-Information:**
- Higher-tier data requires explicit permission
- Respect others' privacy preferences
- Do not pressure others to share tiers they're uncomfortable sharing

**Meeting in Person:**
- Never pressure someone to meet before they're ready
- Always meet in public places for first meetings
- Respect if someone wants to take things slowly

**Consequences:** Pressuring, coercing, or disrespecting boundaries will result in suspension or termination.

---

### 3.3 Prohibited Content in Messages

❌ **You may NOT send:**

**Sexually Explicit Content:**
- Sexually explicit messages, photos, or videos
- Requests for explicit content
- Sexual propositions or solicitations
- Unsolicited descriptions of sexual acts

**Harassment:**
- Repeated unwanted contact
- Insults, name-calling, or degrading comments
- Threats or intimidation
- Stalking or obsessive behavior

**Spam:**
- Commercial solicitations or advertisements
- Pyramid schemes or MLM recruitment
- Links to external websites (without context)
- Copypasta or repetitive messages

**Personal Information (Premature Sharing):**
- Sharing your phone number, email, or address in first message
- Requesting personal contact information before building rapport
- Sharing social media handles before mutual interest

**Note:** Sharing contact information is allowed after established conversation, but use caution.

**Consequences:** First offense = warning. Second offense = 7-day suspension. Third offense = permanent ban.

## 4. Prohibited Conduct

### 4.1 Harassment & Abuse

**Zero tolerance for:**
- Repeatedly contacting someone who declined your interest
- Sending threatening or intimidating messages
- Stalking or obsessive behavior (e.g., viewing someone's profile 50+ times/day)
- Creating new accounts to contact someone who blocked you
- Doxing (sharing someone's private information without consent)

**Consequences:** Immediate account suspension pending investigation. Permanent ban if confirmed.

---

### 4.2 Hate Speech & Discrimination

**Zero tolerance for:**
- Hate speech based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality
- Discriminatory comments or slurs
- Promoting extremist or supremacist ideologies
- Harassment based on protected characteristics

**Examples:**
- ❌ "I don't date [ethnic group]" (discriminatory generalization)
- ✅ "I prefer someone who shares my cultural background" (personal preference)

**Consequences:** First offense = immediate suspension. Severe cases = permanent ban.

---

### 4.3 Sexual Exploitation & Abuse

**Zero tolerance for:**
- Soliciting sexual content or services
- Offering money in exchange for dates or companionship
- Promoting escort services or sex work
- Sharing explicit photos without consent
- Coercing or manipulating others into sexual situations

**Consequences:** Immediate permanent ban. We may report to law enforcement if illegal activity is suspected.

---

### 4.4 Scams & Fraud

**Zero tolerance for:**

**Financial Scams:**
- Requesting money or financial assistance
- Investment or business opportunity solicitations
- Fake emergency requests ("I'm stranded, send money")
- Romance scams (building trust to later request money)

**Identity Fraud:**
- Using stolen photos
- Impersonating another person
- Creating fake profiles for catfishing

**Consequences:** Immediate permanent ban. We cooperate with law enforcement on fraud investigations.

---

### 4.5 Spam & Commercial Activity

**Prohibited:**
- Using JoyMatcher to promote businesses, products, or services
- MLM or pyramid scheme recruitment
- Sending bulk messages with identical content
- Directing users to external websites for commercial purposes
- Using the Platform for lead generation or data harvesting

**Exceptions:** Mentioning your profession (e.g., "I run a small business") is okay. Active promotion is not.

**Consequences:** First offense = warning. Second offense = permanent ban.

---

### 4.6 Platform Manipulation

**Prohibited:**
- Using bots or automation to send messages or interests
- Scraping or data mining user information
- Creating multiple accounts to circumvent bans
- Gaming the tier system (e.g., sharing tier information externally to bypass EDT)
- Coordinating off-platform to bypass Show Interest rules

**Consequences:** Immediate permanent ban. Technical measures will be taken to prevent re-registration.

## 5. Safety Best Practices

### 5.1 Protect Your Personal Information

✅ **DO:**
- Keep conversations on JoyMatcher initially
- Share contact information only when comfortable
- Use video calls before meeting in person (to verify identity)
- Trust your instincts (if something feels off, it probably is)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Share your full name, address, or workplace in early messages
- Share financial information (bank account, credit card)
- Send money to matches (even if they claim emergency)
- Share passwords or login credentials

---

### 5.2 Meeting in Person Safely

✅ **DO:**
- Meet in public places (coffee shops, restaurants, parks)
- Inform a trusted friend or family member of your plans
- Arrange your own transportation
- Stay sober (avoid excessive alcohol)
- Video call before meeting to verify identity

❌ **DON'T:**
- Meet at private residences for first dates
- Let your match pick you up from your home
- Share your live location
- Leave drinks unattended

**If you feel unsafe during a meeting, leave immediately and contact local authorities if necessary.**

---

### 5.3 Recognizing Red Flags

**Warning signs of potential scams or unsafe users:**
- Requests for money (even small amounts)
- Professing love very quickly ("I love you" in first week)
- Reluctance to video call or meet in person
- Vague or evasive answers about their life
- Photos that look like stock images or modeling photos
- Asking to move off-platform immediately
- Pressuring you to share personal information
- Claiming to be in military, overseas, or unable to meet

**If you see red flags, report via [Safety Center](app/safety/report).**

## 6. Tier System Etiquette

### 6.1 Respecting Tier Boundaries

**Remember:**
- Tier 2-5 data is **private** until shared
- You cannot view tiers you haven't completed yourself
- You cannot view tiers the other person hasn't granted access to
- Effective Disclosure Tier (EDT) governs what you both see

✅ **DO:**
- Respect if someone declines to share higher tiers
- Understand that tier sharing takes time and trust
- Be patient with tier negotiation

❌ **DON'T:**
- Pressure others to share tiers prematurely
- Ask about tier information outside the Platform (e.g., "What's your genotype?" before they share Tier 4)
- Share tier information with others outside the Platform
- Use tier information to discriminate or judge

---

### 6.2 Revocation Rights

**Everyone has the right to revoke tier access at any time.**

If someone revokes your access:
- ✅ Respect their decision
- ✅ Move on gracefully
- ❌ Do NOT demand explanations
- ❌ Do NOT harass or retaliate

**Consequences:** Retaliating against revocation will result in suspension or termination.

## 7. Subscription & Upgrade Conduct

### 7.1 Respectful Upgrade Conversations

✅ **DO:**

**VIP members are held to HIGHER standards of conduct.**

**Consequences:** VIP members who violate guidelines may lose VIP status without refund.

## 8. Reporting & Enforcement

### 8.1 How to Report Violations

**In-App Reporting:**
1. Navigate to the user's profile
2. Click "Report User"
3. Select violation type (harassment, spam, inappropriate content, etc.)
4. Provide details and evidence (screenshots, message history)
5. Submit report

**Safety Center:**
- Navigate to [Safety Center](app/safety/report)
- Fill out detailed report form

**Email:**
- safety@joymatcher.com (for urgent safety threats)

---

### 8.2 What Happens After You Report

**Review Process:**
1. Reports are reviewed by moderators within 2-24 hours (urgent cases <2 hours)
2. Moderators assess evidence and context
3. Moderators may contact you for additional information
4. Action is taken if violation is confirmed

**Possible Actions:**
- **Warning:** First-time minor violations
- **Content Removal:** Photos or messages removed
- **Temporary Suspension:** 7, 14, or 30 days
- **Permanent Ban:** Serious or repeated violations
- **Law Enforcement Referral:** Illegal activity (rare)

**You will NOT be notified** of the specific action taken (for privacy reasons).

---

### 8.3 False Reporting

**Do NOT abuse the reporting system.**

False reports:
- Waste moderation resources
- Harm innocent users
- Undermine the safety system

**Consequences:** Users who repeatedly file false reports may be suspended or terminated.

---

### 8.4 Appeals

If your account is suspended or terminated, you may appeal:

**How to Appeal:**
1. Email: appeals@joymatcher.com
2. Include: Your account email, reason for appeal, any evidence
3. Appeals are reviewed by senior moderators within 7 business days

**Grounds for Appeal:**
- Mistaken identity (someone reported the wrong person)
- Context misunderstood by moderator
- Technical error

**Note:** Appeals for serious violations (harassment, fraud, hate speech) are rarely successful.

## 9. Enforcement & Consequences

### 9.1 Violation Severity Levels

**Minor Violations (Warning):**
- Inappropriate photo (not explicit)
- Mildly unprofessional language
- Premature contact information sharing

**Moderate Violations (7-14 Day Suspension):**
- Repeated inappropriate messages
- Pressuring others to share tiers
- Spamming multiple users
- Minor harassment

**Serious Violations (30 Day Suspension or Permanent Ban):**
- Sexually explicit content
- Threats or intimidation
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Creating multiple accounts
- Persistent harassment

**Severe Violations (Immediate Permanent Ban):**
- Sexual exploitation
- Fraud or scams
- Violence or credible threats
- Doxing
- Illegal activity

---

### 9.2 Three-Strike Policy (For Moderate Violations)

1. **First Strike:** Warning + 7-day suspension
2. **Second Strike:** 30-day suspension
3. **Third Strike:** Permanent ban

**Note:** Serious/severe violations skip the three-strike system and result in immediate suspension or ban.

---

### 9.3 Ban Evasion

**Do NOT create new accounts to evade bans.**

Attempting to circumvent a ban will:
- Result in immediate termination of new account
- Extend original ban duration
- Result in IP address blocking
- May result in legal action if fraud or harassment continues

## 11. Off-Platform Conduct

### 11.1 Our Reach & Limitations

**JoyMatcher can only moderate conduct on our Platform.**

Once you move off-platform (phone, WhatsApp, in-person meetings):
- We cannot monitor or moderate interactions
- We cannot intervene in disputes
- We are not liable for off-platform conduct

**However:** If off-platform harassment or threats occur, you may still report via safety@joymatcher.com. We may terminate the offender's account.
