---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

### 1.1 Definition

**Cookies** are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit.

**Example:**
- When you log in to JoyMatcher, a cookie stores your session token so you don't have to log in on every page

### 1.2 Types of Tracking Technologies

We use several tracking technologies:
- **Cookies:** Small text files stored locally
- **Local Storage:** Browser storage for larger data (e.g., user preferences)
- **Session Storage:** Temporary storage cleared when browser closes
- **Pixels:** Tiny images that track page views
- **Web Beacons:** Embedded tracking codes in emails

## 2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies (Can Opt Out)

**Purpose:** Understand user behavior to improve the Platform.

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|----------|---------|----------|------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Tracks unique visitors | 2 years | Analytics |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Tracks session | 24 hours | Analytics |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics | Throttles request rate | 1 minute | Analytics |

**Data Collected:**

**Data NOT Collected:**

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest (business operations, product improvement)

**How to Opt Out:**

## 5. How to Manage Cookies

### 5.1 Via JoyMatcher Settings

**Recommended Method:**
1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to [Account Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences](app/account/privacy)
3. Toggle cookie categories on/off:
   - ✅ Essential (cannot disable)
   - 🔲 Functional
   - 🔲 Analytics
   - 🔲 Marketing
4. Click "Save Preferences"

**Changes take effect immediately.**

---

### 5.2 Via Browser Settings

**Block All Cookies (Not Recommended):**
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies > Block all cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies > Block all cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Block all cookies
- **Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Block all cookies

**⚠️ Warning:** Blocking all cookies will prevent you from logging in to JoyMatcher.

---

### 5.3 Clear Existing Cookies

**Chrome:**
1. Settings > Privacy & Security > Clear browsing data
2. Select "Cookies and other site data"
3. Choose time range (e.g., "Last 7 days")
4. Click "Clear data"

**Firefox:**
1. Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
2. Click "Clear Data"

**Safari:**
1. Preferences > Privacy
2. Click "Manage Website Data"
3. Click "Remove All"

**Edge:**
1. Settings > Cookies and site permissions
2. Click "Manage and delete cookies and site data"
3. Click "See all cookies and site data" > "Remove all"

## 6. Cookie Consent Banner

### 6.1 First-Time Visitors

When you first visit JoyMatcher, you will see a **cookie consent banner:**

```
🍪 JoyMatcher uses cookies to enhance your experience.

We use essential cookies for login and security, and optional cookies for analytics and marketing.

[Accept All] [Reject Optional] [Customize]
```

**Options:**
- **Accept All:** Accepts all cookie categories (essential, functional, analytics, marketing)
- **Reject Optional:** Accepts only essential cookies
- **Customize:** Choose which optional cookies to accept

---

### 6.2 Consent Storage

Your cookie preferences are stored in the `cookie_consent` cookie.

**Example:**
```json
{
  "essential": true,   // Cannot opt out
  "functional": true,
  "analytics": false,
  "marketing": false,
  "consentDate": "2026-02-26T10:00:00Z",
  "version": "1.0.0"
}
```

---

### 6.3 Consent Withdrawal

You may withdraw consent at any time:
- Navigate to [Account Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences](app/account/privacy)
- Toggle cookies off
- Click "Save Preferences"

**Effect:** We will stop setting new cookies of that type. Existing cookies will be deleted.

## 7. Do Not Track (DNT)

### 7.1 What Is DNT?

"Do Not Track" (DNT) is a browser setting that signals websites not to track you.

**How to Enable DNT:**
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Send "Do Not Track" request
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Send "Do Not Track" signal
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Ask websites not to track me

### 7.2 JoyMatcher's DNT Policy

**We respect DNT signals for optional cookies:**
- If DNT is enabled, we will NOT set analytics or marketing cookies
- Essential cookies (login, security) are still required

**Note:** Most websites do not honor DNT due to lack of standard implementation.

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1 Cookie Duration

See Section 3 for specific cookie durations.

**Summary:**
- **Session cookies:** Deleted when you log out or close browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Stored for specified duration (30 days, 1 year, 2 years)

### 8.2 What Happens When Cookies Expire?

- Cookies are automatically deleted by your browser
- We do not manually delete cookies from your device
- Next time you visit, new cookies will be set (if you consent)
