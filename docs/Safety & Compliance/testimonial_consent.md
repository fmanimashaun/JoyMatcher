# Testimonial & Success Story Consent Policy

**Version:** 1.0.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-02-26
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-26

---

## Introduction

When users find successful relationships through JoyMatcher, we may invite them to share their success stories. **All testimonials are entirely voluntary and require explicit consent.**

This document explains:
- How we request testimonials
- What information may be shared
- Your control over your story
- How to withdraw consent

---

## 1. Success Story Process

### 1.1 When We Invite You

We may invite you to share your story if:
- ✅ You reported finding a relationship via [Success Flow](app/success/found-match)
- ✅ You and your partner both matched on JoyMatcher
- ✅ Your relationship has lasted at least 3 months
- ✅ You indicated willingness to be featured (optional checkbox during exit flow)

**Invitation is NOT automatic.** We manually review and selectively invite users whose stories would inspire others.

---

### 1.2 Invitation Process

**Step 1: Initial Contact**
- We email you at your registered email address
- Subject: "Would you like to share your JoyMatcher success story?"
- Email explains:
  - What will be shared (details below)
  - Where it will be published (website, blog, social media)
  - Your right to decline or withdraw consent later

**Step 2: Review & Consent**
- You review the testimonial consent form (linked in email)
- You provide explicit consent by checking boxes and signing digitally

**Step 3: Story Collection**
- We schedule a phone/video call or send a questionnaire
- You share your story in your own words
- We may ask follow-up questions

**Step 4: Draft Review**
- We draft your testimonial based on your responses
- We send you the draft for approval
- You may request edits or withdraw consent

**Step 5: Photo Selection (Optional)**
- You may provide a photo (optional, not required)
- Photo must be approved by you
- See Section 3.3 for photo guidelines

**Step 6: Publication**
- After your final approval, we publish your story
- You receive a link to your published testimonial

---

## 2. What Information May Be Shared

### 2.1 Always Included (With Consent)

**Identifiable Information:**
- ✅ First name (e.g., "Chioma")
- ✅ City or region (e.g., "Lagos" or "Toronto")
- ✅ Age range (e.g., "30s") OR exact age (if you consent)
- ✅ How you met (e.g., "We matched in February 2026")
- ✅ Your success story (in your own words)

**Example Testimonial:**
> "Chioma, 32, Lagos
>
> I met my husband Emmanuel through JoyMatcher in February 2026. As a diaspora Nigerian living in Toronto, I struggled to find someone who understood my dual identity. JoyMatcher's tier system helped me feel safe sharing my values gradually. Emmanuel and I connected over our shared faith and desire for family. We got engaged in July 2026 and married in December. Thank you, JoyMatcher!"

---

### 2.2 Optional (Only If You Consent)

- ⚠️ Last name (e.g., "Chioma Okafor") - most users prefer first name only
- ⚠️ Photo of you (alone or with partner)
- ⚠️ Partner's name and story (requires separate consent from partner)
- ⚠️ Occupation (e.g., "Marketing Manager")
- ⚠️ Wedding date or engagement date
- ⚠️ How many tiers you completed before matching

---

### 2.3 Never Shared (Even With Consent)

**We will NEVER share:**
- ❌ Your email address, phone number, or contact information
- ❌ Your last name (unless you explicitly request it)
- ❌ Tier 3-5 private data (health, family history, income, etc.)
- ❌ Your partner's information without their explicit consent
- ❌ Negative experiences or complaints (testimonials are positive only)
- ❌ Your full profile or photos beyond what you approve

---

## 3. Consent Requirements

### 3.1 Explicit Consent

**You must explicitly consent to:**
1. ✅ Sharing your success story in general
2. ✅ Specific details (name, location, age, story)
3. ✅ Publication locations (website, blog, social media)
4. ✅ Photo usage (if providing photo)

**Consent Form Example:**
```
I, [Your Name], consent to JoyMatcher publishing my success story with the following details:

☐ First name only (Chioma)
☐ Full name (Chioma Okafor)

☐ City only (Lagos)
☐ City and country (Lagos, Nigeria)

☐ Age range (30s)
☐ Exact age (32)

☐ My story (as drafted and approved by me)

☐ Photo (attached separately)

I consent to publication on:
☐ JoyMatcher website (/success-stories)
☐ JoyMatcher blog
☐ JoyMatcher social media (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)

Signature: _______________
Date: _______________
```

**No blanket consent.** You choose exactly what is shared.

---

### 3.2 Partner Consent (If Applicable)

**If your story mentions your partner:**
- Your partner must provide separate consent
- Their consent form is identical to yours
- Both consents are required for publication
- If your partner declines, we will anonymize their details (e.g., "I met my partner through JoyMatcher")

---

### 3.3 Photo Consent (Optional)

**If you provide a photo:**
- ✅ Photo must be of you alone OR you and your partner (both consenting)
- ✅ Photo must be recent and accurately represent you
- ✅ Photo must be appropriate (no explicit, suggestive, or offensive content)
- ❌ No children in photos (for privacy)
- ❌ No third parties (unless they consent separately)

**Photo Rights:**
- You grant JoyMatcher a non-exclusive license to use the photo
- You retain ownership of the photo
- We will credit you (e.g., "Photo courtesy of Chioma O.")

---

## 4. Where Testimonials Are Published

### 4.1 Publication Locations

**With your consent, testimonials may appear on:**
- **JoyMatcher Website:** [/success-stories](success-stories) page
- **JoyMatcher Blog:** Featured in relationship advice articles
- **Social Media:** Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X
- **Marketing Materials:** Brochures, ads, presentations (only if you explicitly consent)

**You control which locations.** You may consent to website only but decline social media, for example.

---

### 4.2 Third-Party Publications

**If a third party (journalist, blogger, media outlet) wants to feature your story:**
- We will contact you for separate consent
- You may decline or negotiate terms directly with the third party
- JoyMatcher does not share your contact information without permission

---

## 5. Withdrawing Consent

### 5.1 Right to Withdraw

**You may withdraw consent at any time, for any reason.**

**No explanation is required.**

---

### 5.2 How to Withdraw

**Email:** testimonials@joymatcher.com
**Subject:** "Withdraw Testimonial Consent"
**Include:**
- Your full name
- Email address used for consent
- Link to your published testimonial (if known)

**We will respond within 2 business days.**

---

### 5.3 Effect of Withdrawal

**Within 7 business days of withdrawal:**
- ✅ Your testimonial is removed from JoyMatcher website
- ✅ Social media posts featuring your story are deleted
- ✅ Your photo is removed from CDN and backups
- ⚠️ Blog posts featuring your story are updated (your details anonymized or removed)
- ⚠️ Third-party publications are outside our control (we will request removal but cannot guarantee)

**Anonymized version (optional):**
- Instead of full removal, you may request anonymization
- We replace your name with "Anonymous" or "A JoyMatcher User"
- We remove your photo and identifiable details
- Your story remains (e.g., "A user from Lagos met their partner through JoyMatcher...")

---

## 6. Compensation

### 6.1 No Monetary Compensation

**Testimonials are voluntary and unpaid.**

We do not provide:
- ❌ Cash payment
- ❌ Gift cards
- ❌ Free subscriptions
- ❌ Referral bonuses

**Exception:** If your story is featured in paid media (e.g., magazine article, TV interview), we may negotiate compensation separately.

---

### 6.2 Non-Monetary Benefits

**As a thank you, we may offer:**
- ✅ Public recognition (your story featured prominently)
- ✅ Link to your social media or website (if you request)
- ✅ JoyMatcher "Success Story" badge on your profile (if still active)

**These are optional.** You may decline.

---

## 7. Editing & Accuracy

### 7.1 Editorial Review

**We reserve the right to:**
- ✅ Edit for clarity, grammar, and length
- ✅ Remove inappropriate language or offensive content
- ✅ Format for consistency (e.g., standardize date format)

**We will NOT:**
- ❌ Change the meaning or tone of your story
- ❌ Add fabricated details
- ❌ Exaggerate or embellish

---

### 7.2 Draft Approval

**You must approve the final draft before publication.**

**If you are not satisfied with the draft:**
- Request edits (we will revise)
- Withdraw consent (we will not publish)

## 8. Privacy & Data Protection

### 8.1 Testimonial Data Storage

**Data we collect for testimonials:**
- Your consent form (signed, with timestamp)
- Your story (text)
- Your photo (if provided)
- Your contact information (for consent verification)

**Retention:**
- **Published testimonials:** Stored indefinitely (unless you withdraw consent)
- **Unpublished testimonials:** Deleted after 90 days if you decline consent
- **Consent forms:** Retained for 7 years (legal compliance)

---

### 8.2 Data Protection Compliance

**Your testimonial data is protected under:**
- [Privacy Policy](privacy_policy.md)
- GDPR (for EU users)
- NDPR (for Nigerian users)
- CCPA (for California users)

**You have the right to:**
- ✅ Access your testimonial data
- ✅ Request correction of inaccuracies
- ✅ Request deletion (withdraw consent)
- ✅ Export your testimonial data

## 9. Testimonial Guidelines

### 9.1 What Makes a Good Testimonial

**We look for testimonials that:**
- ✅ Are authentic and honest
- ✅ Highlight the tier system or specific JoyMatcher features
- ✅ Inspire hope without overpromising
- ✅ Represent diverse user demographics (diaspora, home-based, age ranges, religions)

**Example Good Testimonial:**
> "I appreciated how JoyMatcher's tier system let me share my values gradually. By the time we met in person, we'd already built deep trust."

---

### 9.2 What We Avoid

**We do NOT publish testimonials that:**
- ❌ Overpromise results (e.g., "You'll find your soulmate in 30 days!")
- ❌ Disparage competitors
- ❌ Include inappropriate or offensive content
- ❌ Share private health, financial, or family details
- ❌ Promote products or services unrelated to JoyMatcher

---

## 10. Success Story Integrity

### 10.1 Authenticity

**All testimonials are real and verifiable.**

We do NOT:
- ❌ Create fake testimonials
- ❌ Pay actors to pose as users
- ❌ Exaggerate or fabricate stories

**If we cannot verify your story, we will not publish it.**

---

### 10.2 Verification Process

**Before publication, we verify:**
- ✅ You are a real JoyMatcher user (account exists)
- ✅ You and your partner matched on JoyMatcher (message history confirms)
- ✅ Your relationship lasted at least 3 months (self-reported)

**We do NOT require:**
- ❌ Marriage certificate (not all users marry immediately)
- ❌ Proof of relationship status (we trust self-reporting)

## 11. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 11.1 Can I share my story anonymously?

**Yes.** You may request:
- "Anonymous user from Lagos"
- No photo
- No identifiable details

Your story will still be published, but you remain anonymous.

---

### 11.2 What if my partner doesn't want to be featured?

**No problem.** We will anonymize your partner:
- "I met my partner through JoyMatcher"
- No partner name, photo, or details

Your story can still be published with your consent alone.

---

### 11.3 Can I update my testimonial after publication?

**Yes.** Email testimonials@joymatcher.com with requested changes.

**We will update if:**
- ✅ You want to add/remove details
- ✅ You got married after initial testimonial (update status)
- ✅ You want to add a photo

**We will NOT update if:**
- ❌ Changes alter the meaning significantly (we may remove instead)
- ❌ Changes violate guidelines

---

### 11.4 What if I break up after publishing my testimonial?

**We understand relationships evolve.**

**Options:**
- Withdraw consent → We remove your testimonial
- Anonymize → We keep the story but remove your name
- Leave as-is → Your testimonial remains (it was accurate at the time)

**No judgment.** You decide.

---

### 11.5 Will my testimonial affect my JoyMatcher account?

**No.** Your account status is independent of testimonials.

**You may:**
- Delete your JoyMatcher account (testimonial remains unless you withdraw consent)
- Reactivate your account (testimonial remains)
- Change your profile (testimonial reflects the time of publication)
