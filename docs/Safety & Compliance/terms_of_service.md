## 4. Tier System & Data Disclosure Rules

### 4.1 Tier Completion Requirements

Each tier must be **fully completed** before you can progress. All fields within a tier are mandatory. You cannot skip questions or tiers.

**Tier Definitions:**
- **Tier 1:** Identity & Intent (public for all non-VIP users who haven't set profile to private)
- **Tier 2:** Lifestyle & Background (request-based, Premium eligibility)
- **Tier 3:** Relationships & Family (Premium required)
- **Tier 4:** Health & Compatibility (Premium required, VIP eligibility)
- **Tier 5:** Verified Identity/KYC (VIP only, manual approval required)

### 4.2 Subscription Ceilings

Your subscription determines **maximum tier access:**
- Free: Tiers 1-2 (Tier 2 request-based only)
- Premium: Tiers 2-4
- VIP: Tiers 1-5 (after verification)

You **cannot** complete a tier beyond your subscription level.

### 4.3 Effective Disclosure Tier (EDT)

The information visible between you and another user is governed by the **Effective Disclosure Tier (EDT):**

```
EDT = Math.min(
  YourMaxCompletedTier,
  TheirMaxCompletedTier,
  TierYouSharedWithThem,
  TierTheySharedWithYou
)
```

**Example:** If you completed Tier 4, they completed Tier 3, you shared Tier 3 with them, and they shared Tier 2 with you, the EDT = 2. You both see each other's Tier 1-2 data only.

### 4.4 One-to-One Disclosure

Tier sharing is **pairwise and independent:**
- If you share Tier 3 with User A, that does NOT share Tier 3 with User B
- Each relationship has its own disclosure level
- You must explicitly share tiers with each person

### 4.5 Revocation Rights

You may **revoke tier access at any time:**
- Revocation is **instant and symmetric** (both parties lose access simultaneously)
- Revoked access cannot be re-requested for **3 months** (cooldown period)
- You are not required to provide a reason for revocation

### 4.6 No Tier Circumvention

You may not:
- Share tier information outside the Platform
- Screenshot or copy tier data to share with others
- Request tier information via external channels (email, social media, phone)

Violation results in immediate account suspension or termination.

## 5. Show Interest & Messaging Rules

### 5.1 Show Interest Requirement

You **cannot message** another user unless:
1. You have shown interest in them, AND
2. They have accepted your interest

Unsolicited messaging is prohibited.

### 5.2 Show Interest Eligibility

Your subscription determines who you can show interest to:
- **Free:** Can show interest to other Free users only
- **Premium:** Can show interest to Free and Premium users
- **VIP:** Can show interest to all users (Free, Premium, VIP)

### 5.3 Interest Acceptance

The other user must **accept your interest** before messaging is enabled. They may:
- Accept (messaging unlocked)
- Decline (no messaging, no re-request allowed)
- Ignore (no action, interest remains pending)

### 5.4 Cooldown Period

If a user declines your interest or revokes tier access, you **cannot** show interest again for **3 months**.

### 5.5 Messaging Standards

All messages must comply with [Community Guidelines](community_guidelines.md). Prohibited content includes:
- Harassment, threats, or abusive language
- Sexually explicit content or solicitation
- Spam or commercial solicitation
- Hate speech or discriminatory remarks
- Requests for money or financial information
- Sharing of tier information outside the Platform

### 5.6 Message Monitoring

We do **not** proactively read messages. However, flagged messages may be reviewed by moderators for safety purposes.

### 6.1 VIP Application Process

1. Complete Tier 4 as a Premium user
2. Submit VIP application (18-question intent screening)

5. Upload government-issued ID and complete video verification

7. If verified, you are assigned a matchmaking expert
8. Begin VIP onboarding and concierge service

### 6.3 VIP Expert Assignment

VIP members are assigned a **freelance matchmaking expert** who:
- Conducts onboarding sessions

**VIP Experts can ONLY access your profile if assigned to you.** They have no platform-wide browsing ability.

### 6.4 VIP Privacy Controls

VIP members may:

### 6.5 VIP Satisfaction Standards
