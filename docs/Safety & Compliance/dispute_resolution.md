## Introduction

JoyMatcher is committed to resolving disputes fairly, transparently, and efficiently. This document outlines procedures for resolving conflicts between:
- Users and JoyMatcher (platform disputes)
- Users and other users (interpersonal disputes)
- VIP members and VIP Experts (service disputes)
- Admin actions and affected users (moderation appeals)

---

## 1. Types of Disputes

### 1.1 Platform Disputes (User vs. JoyMatcher)

**Examples:**
- Account suspension or termination
- Billing or refund disputes
- Subscription tier access issues
- Privacy or data concerns
- Accessibility barriers

**Resolution Path:** Internal escalation (Section 2)

---

### 1.2 Interpersonal Disputes (User vs. User)

**Examples:**
- Harassment or abusive behavior
- Tier access revocation conflicts
- Miscommunication or misunderstandings
- Romantic rejection disputes

**Resolution Path:** Mediation (Section 3) or Reporting (Section 4)

---

### 1.3 VIP Service Disputes (VIP Member vs. VIP Expert)

**Examples:**
- Dissatisfaction with introduction quality
- Communication breakdown
- Missed consultation sessions
- Expert performance concerns

**Resolution Path:** VIP escalation (Section 5)

---

### 1.4 Moderation Appeals (User vs. Admin Decision)

**Examples:**
- Account suspension appeal
- Photo rejection appeal
- Report dismissal appeal

**Resolution Path:** Appeals process (Section 6)

---

## 2. Platform Dispute Resolution (Internal Escalation)

### 2.1 Level 1: Self-Service

**For common issues, try self-service first:**
- Billing inquiries: [Account Settings > Billing](app/account/billing)
- Subscription changes: [Account Settings > Subscription](app/account/subscription)
- Profile edits: [Edit Profile](app/profile/me/edit)

---

### 2.2 Level 2: Customer Support

**If self-service doesn't resolve the issue:**

**Email:** support@joymatcher.com
**Subject:** Brief description of issue (e.g., "Billing dispute - duplicate charge")
**Include:**
- Your account email
- Description of issue
- Evidence (screenshots, transaction IDs, etc.)
- Desired resolution

**Response Time:**
- Free users: 48 hours
- Premium users: 24 hours
- VIP users: 12 hours

**Resolution Rate:** ~80% of disputes resolved at this level

---

### 2.3 Level 3: Escalation to Management

**If Customer Support cannot resolve:**

**Email:** escalations@joymatcher.com
**Subject:** "Escalation: [Brief description]"
**Include:**
- Previous support ticket number
- Summary of issue and prior responses
- Why you believe the issue was not resolved fairly

**Review Team:**
- Product Lead
- Customer Success Manager
- Legal Counsel (if legal issue)

**Response Time:** 5 business days

**Resolution Rate:** ~90% of disputes resolved at this level

---

### 2.4 Level 4: Legal Escalation (Arbitration/Court)

**If internal escalation fails, see Section 9 for legal remedies.**

## 3. Interpersonal Dispute Mediation (User vs. User)

### 3.1 When to Seek Mediation

**Mediation is appropriate for:**
- Miscommunication or misunderstandings
- Tier access revocation disputes (e.g., "Why did you revoke my access?")
- Non-threatening conflicts

**Mediation is NOT appropriate for:**
- Harassment or threats (report immediately via [Safety Center](app/safety/report))
- Policy violations (use reporting system, Section 4)
- Criminal activity (contact local law enforcement)

---

### 3.2 Mediation Process

**Step 1: Request Mediation**
- Both parties must agree to mediation
- Email: mediation@joymatcher.com
- Subject: "Mediation Request"
- Include: Names/usernames of both parties, brief description of conflict

**Step 2: Mediator Assignment**
- JoyMatcher assigns a neutral moderator (not involved in the dispute)
- Mediator reviews both parties' accounts and interaction history

**Step 3: Mediated Conversation**
- Mediator facilitates written conversation between parties (via email or in-app)
- Each party explains their perspective
- Mediator clarifies misunderstandings, suggests compromises

**Step 4: Resolution or Closure**
- **Best case:** Parties reach mutual understanding or agreement
- **Neutral case:** Parties agree to disengage (block each other, move on)
- **Worst case:** No resolution; parties advised to use blocking features

**Timeline:** Mediation typically takes 3-7 days

**Cost:** Free for all users

---

### 3.3 Mediation Outcomes

**Possible outcomes:**
- ✅ Mutual apology and renewed communication
- ✅ Agreement to limit interaction (e.g., no further tier sharing)
- ✅ Mutual blocking (amicable separation)
- ⚠️ No resolution (parties remain at odds)

**Mediation does NOT result in:**
- ❌ Forcing one party to grant tier access
- ❌ Forcing one party to accept Show Interest
- ❌ Overriding blocking decisions

## 4. Reporting System (Policy Violations)

### 4.1 When to Report (Not Mediate)

**Use the reporting system for:**
- Harassment, threats, or abusive behavior
- Spam or scams
- Inappropriate photos or content
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Fraudulent profiles
- Any violation of [Community Guidelines](community_guidelines.md)

**Do NOT use mediation for safety threats.**

---

### 4.2 Reporting Process

**Step 1: Submit Report**
- Navigate to the user's profile
- Click "Report User"
- Select violation type (dropdown)
- Provide details and evidence (screenshots, message history)
- Submit report

**Step 2: Moderation Review**
- Reports reviewed by moderation team within:
  - **Urgent (safety threat):** <2 hours
  - **Standard (policy violation):** <24 hours

**Step 3: Action Taken**
- Moderators assess evidence and context
- Possible actions:
  - Warning (first-time minor violations)
  - Content removal (photos, messages)
  - Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
  - Permanent ban (serious or repeated violations)

**Step 4: Outcome Notification**
- You are notified that your report was reviewed
- You are NOT notified of specific action taken (privacy)

---

### 4.3 False Reporting

**Do NOT abuse the reporting system.**

**Consequences for false reporting:**
- First offense: Warning
- Second offense: 7-day suspension
- Third offense: Permanent ban

## 5. VIP Service Dispute Resolution

### 5.1 Level 1: Direct Communication with VIP Expert

**For minor concerns:**
- Contact your VIP Expert directly via in-app messaging or email
- Examples: Rescheduling sessions, clarifying preferences, feedback on introductions

**Most VIP disputes resolve at this level.**

---

### 5.2 Level 2: VIP Coordinator Escalation

**If expert does not resolve issue:**

**Email:** vip@joymatcher.com
**Subject:** "VIP Dispute: [Brief description]"
**Include:**
- Your account email
- VIP Expert's name (first name)
- Description of issue
- Evidence (message screenshots, missed session dates)
- Desired resolution (e.g., expert reassignment, refund, improved service)

**VIP Coordinator Review:**
- Reviews expert performance history
- Reviews your feedback and satisfaction ratings
- Contacts expert for their perspective

**Possible Outcomes:**
- ✅ Expert coaching and performance improvement plan
- ✅ Expert reassignment (you get a new expert)
- ✅ Prorated refund (if expert failed to deliver minimum service)
- ⚠️ No action (if issue was due to miscommunication or unrealistic expectations)

**Response Time:** 3 business days

---

### 5.3 Level 3: Super Admin Review

**If VIP Coordinator does not resolve:**

**Email:** legal@joymatcher.com
**Subject:** "VIP Dispute Escalation"

**Super Admin Review:**
- Final internal review
- Considers all evidence and prior communications
- Makes binding decision

**Response Time:** 7 business days

## 6. Moderation Appeals

### 6.1 When to Appeal

**You may appeal if:**
- Your account was suspended or terminated
- Your photo was rejected (and you believe it meets guidelines)
- Your report was dismissed (and you believe action should have been taken)
- You believe a moderation decision was unfair or incorrect

---

### 6.2 Appeals Process

**Step 1: Submit Appeal**
- Email: appeals@joymatcher.com
- Subject: "Appeal: [Type of decision]"
- Include:
  - Your account email
  - Moderation decision you're appealing (e.g., "Account suspended on 2026-02-20")
  - Why you believe the decision was incorrect
  - Evidence supporting your appeal

**Step 2: Senior Moderator Review**
- Appeals reviewed by senior moderators (not the original moderator)
- Review considers:
  - Original evidence
  - Your appeal evidence
  - Your account history (prior violations)
  - Context and intent

**Step 3: Appeal Decision**
- **Appeal granted:** Decision overturned, account reinstated, or photo approved
- **Appeal denied:** Original decision stands

**Response Time:** 7 business days

**Appeal success rate:** ~20-30% (most moderation decisions are upheld)

---

### 6.3 Grounds for Successful Appeal

**Appeals are more likely to succeed if:**
- ✅ Mistaken identity (report was about a different user)
- ✅ Context was misunderstood (e.g., sarcasm misinterpreted as harassment)
- ✅ Policy was applied incorrectly
- ✅ Technical error (e.g., photo was actually compliant)

**Appeals are unlikely to succeed if:**
- ❌ You violated the policy but disagree with the severity
- ❌ You claim "it was a joke" (intent does not excuse harm)
- ❌ You have prior violations of the same policy

## 7. Refund Disputes

### 7.1 Refund Eligibility

**See [Terms of Service Section 3.7](terms_of_service.md) for full refund policy.**

**Summary:**
- ✅ Technical error (duplicate charge)
- ✅ Service unavailability (>48 hours)
- ✅ Account wrongly terminated by JoyMatcher
- ❌ "I didn't find a match" (not eligible)
- ❌ "I changed my mind" (not eligible)
- ❌ Voluntary cancellation (not eligible)

---

### 7.2 Refund Request Process

**Step 1: Submit Refund Request**
- Email: billing@joymatcher.com
- Subject: "Refund Request"
- Include:
  - Account email
  - Transaction ID or invoice number
  - Reason for refund
  - Evidence (if applicable, e.g., screenshot of duplicate charge)

**Step 2: Billing Review**
- Billing team reviews request within 5 business days
- Verifies eligibility per refund policy
- May request additional information

**Step 3: Refund Decision**
- **Approved:** Refund processed within 7-10 business days (to original payment method)
- **Denied:** Email with explanation

---

### 7.3 Refund Dispute Escalation

**If refund request is denied and you disagree:**

**Email:** escalations@joymatcher.com
**Subject:** "Refund Dispute Escalation"

**Management Review:**
- Product Lead and Finance Lead review
- Final decision within 7 business days
- Decision is binding

## 9. Legal Action (Arbitration & Litigation)

### 9.1 Arbitration (Nigerian Users)

**For Nigerian users, disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration** per [Terms of Service Section 16](terms_of_service.md).

**Arbitration Rules:**
- Governed by Arbitration and Conciliation Act (Nigeria)
- Conducted in Lagos, Nigeria
- One arbitrator (mutually agreed, or appointed by Lagos Court of Arbitration)
- Decision is final and binding

**How to Initiate Arbitration:**
1. Attempt internal resolution (Sections 2-7) first
2. Send written notice of arbitration demand to: legal@joymatcher.com
3. Include: Description of dispute, desired resolution, evidence
4. JoyMatcher has 30 days to respond
5. If no resolution, proceed to arbitration appointment

**Cost:** Each party bears their own costs (unless arbitrator awards costs to prevailing party)

---

### 9.2 Litigation (International Users)

**For non-Nigerian users:**
- Disputes resolved in courts of your jurisdiction
- Governed by laws of your country

**Exception:** Injunctive relief (e.g., restraining order) may be sought in any court with jurisdiction.

---

### 9.3 Class Action Waiver

**You agree to resolve disputes individually, not as part of a class action** per [Terms of Service Section 16.4](terms_of_service.md).

**This means:**
- You cannot join a class-action lawsuit against JoyMatcher
- You cannot bring disputes on behalf of others

**Exception:** This waiver may not be enforceable in some jurisdictions (e.g., California has limits on class action waivers).

## 10. Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)

### 10.1 Mediation (Before Arbitration)

**We encourage mediation before arbitration:**
- Faster and less expensive than arbitration
- Non-binding (either party can walk away)
- Confidential

**How to Request Mediation:**
- Email: mediation@joymatcher.com
- We will suggest a neutral mediator (e.g., Lagos Multi-Door Courthouse)

---

### 10.2 Negotiated Settlement

**We are open to negotiated settlements:**
- Good for both parties (avoid legal fees, time delay)
- Confidential

**Contact:** legal@joymatcher.com

## 11. Dispute Prevention Best Practices

### 11.1 For All Users

**Prevent disputes by:**
- ✅ Reading [Terms of Service](terms_of_service.md) and [Community Guidelines](community_guidelines.md) carefully
- ✅ Communicating respectfully with matches
- ✅ Setting realistic expectations (JoyMatcher does not guarantee results)
- ✅ Using blocking features promptly if uncomfortable
- ✅ Reporting policy violations immediately

---

### 11.2 For VIP Members

**Prevent VIP disputes by:**
- ✅ Communicating openly with VIP Expert about preferences
- ✅ Providing honest feedback on introductions
- ✅ Attending scheduled consultation sessions
- ✅ Understanding that VIP does not guarantee finding a spouse

---

### 11.3 For Admins & Moderators

**Prevent moderation disputes by:**
- ✅ Following moderation guidelines consistently
- ✅ Documenting decisions with evidence
- ✅ Providing clear explanations for suspensions/bans
- ✅ Offering appeals process transparency
