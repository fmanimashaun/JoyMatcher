## 1. VIP Membership Overview

- ✅ Access to all 5 tiers (including Tier 5 Verified Identity/KYC)
- ✅ Dedicated freelance matchmaking expert

- A guarantee of finding a spouse or partner

## 2. VIP Application Process

### 2.1 Eligibility Requirements

To apply for VIP membership, you must:
- ✅ Have an active **Premium subscription**
- ✅ Have completed **Tier 4** (Health & Compatibility)
- ✅ Be **serious about marriage** (not casual dating)
- ✅ Be **at least 18 years of age**

---

### 2.2 Application Steps

**Step 1: Submit Application**
- Complete 18-question VIP application questionnaire
- Questions assess:
  - Relationship intent and goals
  - Timeline for marriage
  - Emotional readiness
  - Compatibility with VIP service model
  - Willingness to engage in coaching
  - Flexibility with match criteria

**Step 2: VIP Coordinator Review**
- Application reviewed by JoyMatcher VIP Coordinator
- Review typically takes 3-5 business days
- Criteria assessed:
  - Clarity of intent
  - Realistic expectations
  - Coachability
  - Commitment level

**Step 3: Approval or Denial**
- You will be notified via email and in-app notification
- If **approved:** Proceed to Tier 5 verification
- If **denied:** Retain Premium membership at no additional cost
  - Denial does not reflect on your character or eligibility for relationships
  - Common reasons: Unclear intent, unrealistic expectations, not ready for VIP-level investment

**Step 4: Tier 5 Verification (If Approved)**
- Upload government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, national ID)
- Complete video verification (record 30-second video stating your name and intent)
- VIP Coordinator reviews documents within 24-48 hours
- If verified: VIP membership activated

**Step 5: Expert Assignment**
- You are assigned a freelance matchmaking expert
- Expert conducts onboarding session (60-90 minutes)
- Expert begins curated search

### 2.3 Application Fee

**There is NO application fee.**

If your application is denied, you are not charged.

JoyMatcher reserves the right to deny VIP applications at our sole discretion.

## 3. VIP Expert Assignment

### 3.1 What Is a VIP Expert?

VIP Experts are **freelance matchmaking professionals** (not JoyMatcher employees) who:
- Conduct personalized matchmaking searches
- Create curated introduction proposals
- Provide relationship coaching
- Guide you through the matching process
- Track your satisfaction and progress

**VIP Experts are:**
- ✅ Trained in matchmaking methodology
- ✅ Background-checked and vetted
- ✅ Bound by strict confidentiality agreements (NDAs)
- ✅ Performance-reviewed quarterly

---

### 3.2 Expert-Client Relationship

**Your VIP Expert will:**
- Conduct initial onboarding session (60-90 minutes)
- Search JoyMatcher database for compatible matches on your behalf
- Present 2-4 curated introductions per month
- Facilitate first conversations between you and matches
- Provide feedback and coaching after each introduction
- Schedule follow-up consultation sessions

**Your Responsibilities:**
- Respond to expert communications within 48 hours
- Attend scheduled consultation sessions (or reschedule with 24 hours notice)
- Provide honest feedback on introductions
- Engage in coaching with openness
- Inform expert of any changes in preferences or circumstances

---

### 3.3 Expert Reassignment

You may request expert reassignment if:
- You feel the expert does not understand your needs
- You experience communication issues
- You are dissatisfied with match quality

**How to Request Reassignment:**
- Email: vip@joymatcher.com
- Subject: "VIP Expert Reassignment Request"
- Include: Reason for reassignment (optional, but helpful)

**Reassignment Process:**
- VIP Coordinator reviews request within 3 business days
- You are assigned a new expert within 7 days
- New expert conducts fresh onboarding session

**Limits:** You may request reassignment up to 2 times in a 12-month period. Frequent reassignments may indicate VIP is not the right fit.

---

### 3.4 Expert Performance Standards

VIP Experts are evaluated on:
- **Introduction acceptance rate** (target: >60%)
- **Client satisfaction rating** (target: >4.5/5.0)

If your expert underperforms, we will proactively reassign you.

## 7. VIP Expert Confidentiality

### 7.1 Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

All VIP Experts sign strict NDAs prohibiting:
- Sharing client information with anyone (including other experts)
- Using client data for personal purposes
- Discussing client details outside of JoyMatcher context

**Violations result in immediate termination and legal action.**

---

### 7.2 What Your Expert Can See

**Your VIP Expert can access:**
- ✅ Your full profile (Tiers 1-5) - you are their assigned client
- ✅ Your VIP intake questionnaire
- ✅ Your search preferences
- ✅ Your feedback on introductions
- ✅ **Tier 1-2 data of ALL platform users** (to search for compatible matches)
- ✅ **Tier 3-5 data of potential matches** (ONLY if those users consent to share)

**Your VIP Expert CANNOT access:**
- ❌ Your messages with matches (private)
- ❌ Your payment information
- ❌ Other VIP clients (only you - complete isolation between experts)
- ❌ Tier 3-5 data of potential matches WITHOUT their explicit consent
- ❌ Platform-wide analytics or financial data
- ❌ Moderation logs or reports

**Important:** When your expert finds potential matches for you, they will request permission from those users to view their Tier 3-5 data. Those users can approve/decline and control which tier level to share. This ensures consent-based matching while allowing your expert to assess deeper compatibility.

---

### 7.3 Data Isolation (Technical)

VIP Experts log in to a separate **VIP Expert Portal** (not the main admin panel).

**Database queries are filtered by `expertId` + `assignedClientIds`:**
```javascript
// VIP Expert can ONLY query assigned clients
async function getMyClients(expertId) {
  const assignments = await VIPAssignment.find({
    expertId: expertId,
    status: 'active'
  });

  const assignedClientIds = assignments.map(a => a.vipUserId);

  return await User.find({
    _id: { $in: assignedClientIds }
  });
}
```

**Attempting to access unassigned clients returns 403 Forbidden.**
